Bat

Bats can eat up to 1,200 mosquitoes in an hour. Some bats eat fruit or nectar and can play an important role as pollinators.

  • Scientific name : Chiroptera
  • Hibernation period : 183 days
  • Phylum : Chordata
  • Class : Mammalia

Bats are mammals of the order Chiroptera; with their forelimbs adapted as wings, they are the only mammals naturally capable of true and sustained flight.Bats are the only mammals capable of sustained flight, as opposed to gliding, as in the flying squirrel.
